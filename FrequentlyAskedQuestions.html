Frequently Asked Questions

Click here for the rubric: Summer Reading Assessment Criteria

Where do I get my Summer Reading book? Current BSHS students: summer reading books will be pre-checked out to students through the BSHS library. Each academy will have a distribution event and any books not picked up at this time will be located with Ms. Begley in the library. Incoming freshman: Summer reading books will be pre-checked out to you and sent to your middle school. If you do not receive a book, books can be checked out at Bryan Station High School in the library.

What is the Summer Reading Project Based Learning (PBL) Day? The Summer Reading Project Based Learning (PBL) Day is an academy learning event where students engage in a cross-curricular learning interactive experience with their academies. Students will immerse themselves in the themes of their academy summer reading book while building academy identity and culture.

Where can I find my Summer Reading assignment? Summer reading work for your academy can be found in the individual Academy pages.

When should I finish reading my book? You should be finished reading your book by Thursday, August 22, 2019 for the Summer Reading Project Based Learning (PBL) Day.

When is the Summer Reading Assignment due? Your summer reading work is due to your homeroom teacher on the morning of Thursday, August 22, 2019 –the day of the Summer Reading Project Based Learning (PBL) Day.

When and where do I turn in my summer reading assignment? You will turn in your summer reading assignment to your homeroom teacher on Thursday, August 22, 2019 –the day of the Summer Reading Project Based Learning (PBL) Day.

Where and when do I turn in my Summer Reading book? All books will be collected through homeroom. Students should turn their books into their homeroom teacher on Thursday, August 22, 2019.

Is the Summer Reading assignment and Summer Reading Project Based Learning (PBL) day a performance grade? Yes. You will receive grades in multiple classes, including English and your academy electives for your summer reading work.

Can I reassess my summer reading assignment? Yes. Specific Academy reassessment information can be found at the academy pages.
